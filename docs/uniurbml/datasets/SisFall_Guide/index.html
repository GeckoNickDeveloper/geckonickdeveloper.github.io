SisFall

SisFall Datasets Guide

SisFall is a dataset for Human Activity Recognition (HAR), sampled at 200Hz containing accelerometer and gyroscope data. The following file contains data obtained by a total of 38 subjects: 23 adults (19 - 30 years) and 15 elderly (60 - 75 years)

All data is represented as float64

Acquisition details

In this document, the following notation is used: - Acc. 0: refers to accelerometer ADXL345 (± 16g range) - Gyro.: refers to gyroscope ITG3200 (± 2000°/s range) - Acc. 1: refers to accelerometer MMA8451Q (± 8g range)

The dataset file

SisFall-original.npy

Each record is composed by:

Acc. 0 Gyro. Acc. 1 Label Subject Age Height Weight Gender
x - y - z x - y - z x - y - z label-id subject-id age height weight gender

Activities

Label Activity
0 Walking slowly
1 Walking quickly
2 Jogging slowly
3 Jogging quickly
4 Walking upstairs and downstairs slowly
5 Walking upstairs and downstairs quickly
6 Slowly sit in a half height chair, wait a moment, and up slowly
7 Quickly sit in a half height chair, wait a moment, and up quickly
8 Slowly sit in a low height chair, wait a moment, and up slowly
9 Quickly sit in a low height chair, wait a moment, and up quickly
10 Sitting a moment, trying to get up, and collapse into a chair
11 Sitting a moment, lying slowly, wait a moment, and sit again
12 Sitting a moment, lying quickly, wait a moment, and sit again
13 Being on one's back change to lateral position, wait a moment, and change to one's back
14 Standing, slowly bending at knees, and getting up
15 Standing, slowly bending without bending knees, and getting up
16 Standing, get into a car, remain seated and get out of the car
17 Stumble while walking
18 Gently jump without falling (trying to reach a high object)

SisFall-reduced.npy

This version differs from the original in the following ways: - Removed subjects: from 32 to 37 - Removed quick activities: 1, 3, 5, 7, 9, 12, 17, 18 - Downsampled to 50Hz (linear interpolation used)

Each record is composed by:

x - y - z x - y - z label-id subject-id age height weight gender

Import

In order to import the dataset, you just need to type:

import numpy as np
dataset = np.load('SisFall-reduced.npy')
